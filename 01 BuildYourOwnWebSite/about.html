About Us

Bubble Under is a group of diving enthusiasts based in south-west UK who meet for diving trips in the summer when the weather is good and bacon rolls are flowing. We arrange weekends away as small groups to cut the costs of accommodation and travel, and to ensure that everyone gets a trustworthy dive buddy.

Although we're based in the south-west, we don't stay on our turf: past diving weekends have included trips up to Scapa Flow in Scotland and to Malta's numerous wreck sites.

When we're not diving we often meet up in a local pub to talk about our recent adventures (any excuse, eh?).

Or as our man Bob Dobalina would put it:

"Happiness is a dip in the ocean followed by a pint or two of Old Speckled Hen. You can quote me on that!"
